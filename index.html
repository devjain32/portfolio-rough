I am

My name is Dev Jain, and I am a student at the University of Texas at Austin (Class of 2022). I am currently doing a double major with mechanical engineering and economics. During the summer of 2019, I took a coding bootcamp that taught full stack web development. This included both front- and back-end coding. I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node, MySQL, Ruby, and more. This website was fully coded by me using what I learned at the bootcamp and on my own. It serves as a portfolio of the work I've done. It includes my full updated resume, all of the websites and programs I've coded (both as a freelance web developer and my own projects), research I have done, and other artistic hobbies such as photography and guitar. I hope this sheds some light on my interests and work experience. Thank you!
